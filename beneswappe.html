    • BeneSwappe Design

    Generating value from underutilized items


    BeneSwappe is an online clothing swap service that lets groups of friends, coworkers, students, etc. to easily swap clothes that they do not want anymore. More than 15 million tons of textile are wasted each year in the US. While donating old clothing is a good option, very few of the donated clothes actually make it into the hands of someone in need.

    BeneSwappe is dedicated to generating value from items that would otherwise have been thrown away, while making it easy to swap clothes with your friends.

    Design

    The Solution

    Problem: How might we generate value from unwanted clothing and items
    Users: Anyone who is cleaning out their closet
