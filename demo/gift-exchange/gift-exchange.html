    ABOUT

    Easy-to-use

    Gift Exchange is easy-to-use. Simply create a group to generate a unique link to send out to your peeps!



    For Everyone

    Anyone can use it. All they need is an email address. Everything is done through simple email exchanges.



    Wishlists Included

    All inclusive tool for drawing that even includes adding a wishlist for yourself so no one is left guessing what you want.

    HOW IT WORKS

    Create a group

    One of your members will create the group. This will generate a link to send to everyone who wants to join.

    Click to join

    Group members will click the link and register to join the gift exchange. They can also add a wishlist at this point.

    Draw!

    On the set drawing date, names will be drawn and emailed to each group member. Then it's time to buy gifts!

    FAQ


    Is there a limit to the number of people I can add?

    Nope! You can add as many as you want.


    What if I forgot to copy the link?

    Check your email! We sent a copy of it so that you can always have it.


    How do I edit the details of the gift exchange?

    If you are the creator, you can go to the manage group page and click edit to change any of the details that you want.


    How do I remove someone from the group?

    If you are the creator, you can go to the manage group page and click "x" next to the person's name to remove.


    Can I change the date of the name drawing?

    Yup! If you are the creator, you can go to the manage group page and click edit to change any of the details that you want.


    Is there somewhere I can give feedback about this site?

    Absolutely! Click "Contact Us" below to send an email.
